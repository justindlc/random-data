						<div class="content">
							<div id="html-1" class="mce "><p>Our library provides access to a wealth of electronic resources for scholarly research, instruction and non-commercial use by authorized&nbsp;Atlanta University Center students, faculty and staff.&nbsp;</p>
<p>You may access these resources from within the Library, on an Atlanta University Center campus, or off-campus. Users will need to log in using their <a href="https://login.ezproxy.auctr.edu:2050/login">campus-issued username and password</a>.&nbsp;If you have trouble logging in to your campus account, contact your&nbsp;<a href="http://loginsupport.auctr.edu/" target="_blank">campus&#039;s service desk</a>.</p>
<p>Visitors to the Atlanta University Center may use these resources if such use is non-commercial. A limited number of visitor workstations are available for use. Visit the Information Services Center on the Library&rsquo;s main level to obtain access to a visitor workstation.</p></div>
